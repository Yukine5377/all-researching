**4. Recommendation Engines:**
- **Collaborative Filtering:** Goi y dua tren hanh vi nguoi dung tuong tu
- **Content-Based:** Goi y dua tren dac diem mon an (nguyen lieu, loai, vung mien)

## Regulatory & Legal Focus (Phap ly & Quy dinh)

### 1. Ban quyen noi dung (Copyright)

**Cong thuc nau an & Copyright:**
- Cong thuc nau an (danh sach nguyen lieu + chi dan co ban) **thuong KHONG duoc bao ho ban quyen** theo luat My va nhieu nuoc. ([Vermont Law Review](https://lawreview.vermontlaw.edu/recipes-intellectual-property-and-recipes-in-the-age-of-social-media/))
- Tuy nhien, **phan dien dat sang tao** (cach viet, story, tip ca nhan) CO THE duoc bao ho
- **Video** luon duoc bao ho ban quyen thuoc ve creator va nen tang

**Giai phap cho app:**
- Chi trich xuat **structured data** (nguyen lieu, so luong, buoc lam) - khong sao chep van ban goc
- Luon **link ve video goc** (attribution/credit)
- Khong tai va luu tru video trong app
- Hien thi nhu "Inspired by [Creator Name]" voi link

### 2. Chinh sach API cua cac nen tang MXH

**TikTok:**
- TikTok **cam scraping/crawling** du lieu ma khong co su dong y bang van ban. ([TikTok ToS](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/us/terms-of-service/en))
- TikTok Research API chi cho muc dich nghien cuu, khong cho thuong mai. ([TikTok Research API ToS](https://www.tiktok.com/legal/page/global/terms-of-service-research-api/en))
- **Phuong an:** Nhan URL qua Share Extension -> truy cap public page -> trich xuat caption/metadata (khong scrape noi dung private)

**YouTube:**
- YouTube Data API v3 cho phep truy cap metadata (title, description, captions) theo quota
- **Khong duoc** tai video/audio truc tiep (vi pham ToS)
- **Phuong an:** Dung oEmbed API de lay metadata, dung YouTube captions API de lay subtitles (neu co)

**Facebook/Instagram:**
- Graph API han che cho business accounts
- Public content co the truy cap qua oEmbed
- **Phuong an:** Nhan URL -> oEmbed metadata -> AI xu ly

### 3. Bao mat du lieu ca nhan (Data Privacy)

**Viet Nam:**
- **Nghi dinh 13/2023/ND-CP** ve bao ve du lieu ca nhan (co hieu luc tu 01/07/2023)
- Can su dong y cua nguoi dung truoc khi thu thap du lieu ca nhan (mood, preference, vi tri)
- Phai co chinh sach quyen rieng tu ro rang

**Quoc te:**
- **GDPR** (EU): Neu co user chau Au, can tuan thu
- **CCPA** (California): Neu co user My
- **Apple App Tracking Transparency**: Can xin phep truoc khi tracking

### 4. Quy dinh ve AI

- **EU AI Act** (2024): Recommendation system co the thuoc loai "limited risk" -> can minh bach ve viec dung AI
- Can thong bao nguoi dung rang noi dung duoc AI trich xuat va co the chua chinh xac
- Cho phep nguoi dung chinh sua va xoa du lieu AI xu ly
