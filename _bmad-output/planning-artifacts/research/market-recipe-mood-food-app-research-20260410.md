## Customer Behavior and Segments

### Hanh vi khach hang (Customer Behavior Patterns)

**Thoi quen luu cong thuc tu mang xa hoi:**

Nguoi dung hien tai luu cong thuc nau an tu mang xa hoi bang nhieu cach khac nhau: bookmark tren TikTok/Instagram, screenshot, gui qua tin nhan cho ban be, hoac ghi chu vao ung dung Notes. Tuy nhien, cac cach nay deu co han che lon ve kha nang tim kiem va phan loai lai.

- **56% Gen Z** su dung TikTok de ho tro nau an, so voi chi **29% Millennials** thich YouTube hon (66% su dung YouTube). ([Morning Consult](https://pro.morningconsult.com/analysis/gen-z-social-media-restaurants-recipes))
- **45% Gen Z** noi rang ho bi anh huong boi mang xa hoi lan cuoi cung ho thu mot cong thuc moi. ([Morning Consult](https://pro.morningconsult.com/analysis/gen-z-social-media-restaurants-recipes))
- Tai luong recipe app tang **52%** tai My trong nam 2025. ([ElectroIQ](https://electroiq.com/stats/recipe-app-statistics/))
- Noi dung thuc pham tren TikTok dat ty le tuong tac **6-8%**, cho thay muc do quan tam cao. ([Amra and Elma](https://www.amraandelma.com/top-tiktok-engagement-statistics/))

**Xu huong tieu thu noi dung am thuc:**

- "Short-form economy" (TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels) uoc tinh dat **$40 ty** trong nam 2025 va du kien tang **60%/nam** trong 5 nam toi. ([SQ Magazine](https://sqmagazine.co.uk/tiktok-usage-statistics/))
- Tim kiem "TikTok food trends" dat dinh vao thang 8/2025 (chi so 95) va thang 12/2025 (chi so 70), cho thay tinh mua vu cao. ([Accio](https://www.accio.com/business/tiktok-food-trends-2026))
- **88%** doanh nghiep nho bao cao quang cao TikTok dan den doanh so cao hon, nganh F&B tao ra **$6.4 ty** doanh thu tu TikTok. ([Accio](https://www.accio.com/business/tiktok-food-trends))

_Behavior Drivers: Tiet kiem thoi gian, kham pha mon moi, anh huong tu influencer_
_Interaction Preferences: Video ngan (<60s), visual-first, de lam theo_
_Decision Habits: Xem video -> Bookmark/Screenshot -> Quyen di sieu thi -> Nau theo_

### Ho so tam ly hoc (Psychographic Profiles)

**Gia tri va loi song:**

- **Tiet kiem:** 35% nguoi dung noi ly do hang dau nau an tai nha la de tiet kiem tien, so voi 26% vi dinh duong. ([Instacart Survey](https://www.instacart.com/company/ideas/cooking-at-home-survey))
- **Suc khoe:** Nguoi tieu dung ngay cang dung recipe app de quan ly calo, kiem soat can nang, va dap ung che do an dac biet (plant-based, keto, gluten-free). ([Straits Research](https://straitsresearch.com/report/recipe-apps-market))
- **Kham pha:** Gen Z va Millennials coi am thuc nhu mot hinh thuc giai tri va kham pha van hoa, khong chi la nhu cau an uong.
- **Ban be & Gia dinh:** Ban be va gia dinh van la nguon cam hung nau an so 1 (41%), nhung YouTube (36%) va MXH dang bat kip. ([Home Run Inn Pizza](https://www.homeruninnpizza.com/news-blog/fun-facts/cooking-habits-gen-z-millennials/))

**Tam ly ve mood va thuc pham (Psychology of Mood-Food Connection):**

- Tam trang tich cuc khien nguoi dung huong toi muc tieu dai han (suc khoe) -> **thich do an lanh manh**. ([ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1057740814000060))
- Tam trang tieu cuc khien nguoi dung tap trung vao muc tieu tuc thi (comfort) -> **thich do an "nuong chieu" (indulgent)**. ([ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1057740814000060))
- He thong goi y theo mood dat **85% do chinh xac** va cai thien **20% su hai long** cua khach hang. ([IEEE Xplore](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9545065))

## Customer Pain Points & Nhu cau chua duoc dap ung

### Pain Points khi luu cong thuc tu MXH

**1. Cong thuc phan tan, kho tim lai (Scattered Storage)**
- Nguoi dung "chim" trong bookmark, screenshot, va ghi chu roi rac tren nhieu nen tang. Tim lai mot cong thuc cu the tro thanh "cuoc di san" mat thoi gian. ([Pluck Recipes](https://pluckrecipes.com/best-recipe-app/))
- Bookmark tren TikTok lan voi cac video khac (hai huoc, tin tuc), khong co cach loc rieng "do an"
- Moi nen tang co he thong luu rieng: TikTok Collections, YouTube Save, Instagram Saved, Facebook Saved

**2. Nhap lieu thu cong (Manual Data Entry)**
- Cac app recipe truyen thong (Paprika) yeu cau nhap thu cong hoac chi ho tro clip tu website, khong ho tro video. ([Plan to Eat](https://www.plantoeat.com/blog/2023/07/paprika-app-review-pros-and-cons/))
- Ngay ca Flavorish gioi han **5 luot free/ngay** cho AI extraction. ([Android Police](https://www.androidpolice.com/i-tried-viral-recipe-apps-clear-winner/))

**3. Chat luong trich xuat AI chua hoan hao**
- AI co the sai nguyen lieu, sai so luong, hoac bo sot buoc
- Don vi do Viet Nam mo ho: "mot it", "vua an", "1 nhi", "1 muong ca phe day" — AI kho chuan hoa
- Video khong co caption/description chi tiet -> AI phai dua vao audio, do chinh xac giam

**4. Khong co danh sach di cho tu dong**
- Sau khi luu cong thuc, nguoi dung van phai tu ghi danh sach nguyen lieu
- Khong co tinh nang gop nguyen lieu tu nhieu cong thuc thanh 1 danh sach mua sam

**5. Khong phan biet duoc Recipe vs Review**
- Video tren TikTok co the vua la cong thuc vua la review quan an
- Hien chua co app nao xu ly tot ca hai loai noi dung trong cung mot san pham

### Pain Points khi tim dia diem an uong

**6. Thong tin dia diem khong co cau truc**
- Xem review quan an tren TikTok -> chi nho "quan nay ngon" nhung khong nho ten/dia chi
- Google Maps luu dia diem nhung khong lien ket voi video review goc
- Foody/ShopeeFood chi tap trung giao hang, khong phai kham pha tu review

**7. Khong co goi y theo tam trang (Mood)**
- Tat ca app hien tai goi y theo category (mon Viet, mon Han...) hoac vi tri
- Chua co app nao hoi "hom nay ban cam thay the nao?" roi goi y phu hop
- Nghien cuu cho thay mood anh huong **truc tiep** den lua chon thuc pham. ([ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1057740814000060))

### Nhu cau chua duoc dap ung (Unmet Needs)

| Nhu cau | Hien trang | Co hoi cho app |
|:--|:--|:--|
| Luu cong thuc tu video MXH 1 cham | Bookmark roi rac | Share Extension + AI tu dong |
| Chuan hoa nguyen lieu tieng Viet | Khong co | NLP + food knowledge base VN |
| Goi y mon theo mood | Khong co app nao lam | Mood engine + preference |
| Ket hop recipe + review dia diem | 2 loai app rieng biet | Mot app lam ca hai |
| Danh sach di cho tu dong | Chi co o app tra phi | AI trich xuat -> grocery list |

## Customer Decision Factors (Yeu to quyet dinh cua khach hang)

### Yeu to uu tien khi chon recipe app

**1. Thong tin dinh duong (50% nguoi dung)**
- Phan nua nguoi dung uu tien app co thong tin dinh duong chi tiet (calo, macro, vi chat). ([ElectroIQ](https://electroiq.com/stats/recipe-app-statistics/))

**2. Tinh nang thuc te (Grocery list, Timer)**
- Danh sach di cho trong app anh huong **38% quyet dinh mua sam**. ([ElectroIQ](https://electroiq.com/stats/recipe-app-statistics/))
- Timer va huong dan bang giong noi tang tuong tac **42%**.

**3. Ca nhan hoa (Personalization)**
- Nguoi dung muon app hoc khau vi cua ho va goi y phu hop
- "Cang dung nhieu, cang hieu toi" la ky vong co ban

**4. Giao dien don gian, de dung**
- Doi tuong da dang (noi tro, sinh vien, nguoi lon tuoi) -> UI phai super accessible
- Hinh anh lon, dep, mon an hap dan la yeu to thu hut hang dau. ([Tubik Studio](https://blog.tubikstudio.com/case-study-recipes-app-ux-design/))

**5. Gia ca hop ly**
- 60% dung mien phi, 35% san sang tra phi neu co gia tri ro rang
- Flavorish $4.99/thang, ReciMe $9.99/thang, Paprika $4.99 mua 1 lan
- Mo hinh freemium voi gioi han so luot AI la pho bien nhat

## Tong hop va Khuyen nghi Chien luoc

### Thi truong muc tieu

**Primary:** Social Foodies / Gen Z (18-27 tuoi, global, TikTok-heavy)
**Secondary:** Busy Planners (28-40 tuoi, nau an tai nha, YouTube)
**Tertiary:** Foodie Explorers Vietnam (22-35 tuoi, thanh thi VN)

### Khuyen nghi MVP

**Phase 1 (MVP - 3 thang):**
1. Share Extension nhan link tu TikTok + Facebook Reels + Instagram Reels
2. AI trich xuat cong thuc (caption + audio via Whisper)
3. Chuan hoa nguyen lieu (tieng Viet + da ngon ngu)
4. Phan loai danh muc tu dong
5. Thu vien ca nhan co tim kiem (list view)

**Phase 2 (3-6 thang):**
1. Mood input (emoji) + recommendation engine (rule-based truoc)
2. Luu dia diem tu video review
3. Grocery list tu dong
4. Them YouTube support
5. AI auto-detect mood tu ngu canh

**Phase 3 (6-12 thang):**
1. ML-based recommendation (collaborative filtering)
2. Meal planning theo tuan
3. Tich hop Google Maps cho dia diem
4. Nang cao personalization AI
5. On-device AI cho basic extraction

### Mo hinh kinh doanh de xuat

**Freemium:**
- Free: 3 luot luu/ngay, thu vien co ban, tim kiem co ban
- Premium ($4.99/thang hoac $39.99/nam): Khong gioi han, mood recommendation, grocery list, offline, sync
- Doanh thu bo sung: Affiliate voi nha hang/quan an duoc goi y (Phase sau)

| KPI | Muc tieu 3 thang | Muc tieu 6 thang |
|:--|:--:|:--:|
